Mechanical Engineering

Vision

To dispense pre-eminent education and to enhance the technical skills of students, imparting knowledge in Mechanical Engineering with an industrial focus while fostering innovation and ethics.

Mission

M1: "To prepare students with strong foundational concepts using technology-enhanced tools and techniques, enhancing their understanding of principles, concepts, and latest trends in Mechanical Engineering."

M2: "To provide opportunities that encourage organizational leadership qualities through workshops and laboratories aligned with the State Board of Technical Education, Andhra Pradesh."

M3: "To conduct guest lectures, industrial visits, and training sessions for a deeper understanding of critical Mechanical Engineering concepts."

M4: "To facilitate opportunities for multidisciplinary skill development, communication skills, professional attitude, and ethics, fostering societal and environmental responsibility."
