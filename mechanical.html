Mechanical Engineering


Vision

To dispense pre eminence education and to enhance technical skills of the students pertaining knowledge in Mechanical Engineering with basis to mould the students for Industrial needs and to motivate to be innovative and ethical as well.


Mission

M1: "To Prepare the students with strong foundational concepts by using technology enhanced tools and techniques for enhancement of knowledge, understanding of principles, concepts and latest trends in Mechanical Engineering."
M2: "To Provide opportunities to encourage organizational leadership qualities of students through workshops and laboratories as per the curriculum specified by the State Board of Technical Education, Andhra Pradesh."
M3: "To conduct Guest lectures, Industrial visits and Industrial training for better understanding of critical concepts of Mechanical Engineering to prove their ability."
M4: "To facilitate opportunities for developing multidisciplinary skills, communication skills, professional attitude and ethics, to serve the society and to protect environment as well."
