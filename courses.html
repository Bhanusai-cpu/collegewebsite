Admission criteria

Loyola Polytechnic (YSRR), Pulivendla is a Christian Minority Institution which is primarily (30% of seats) meant for Christian students.
It is also open to others for the rest of seats without distinction of caste, creed or religion according to Government rules in force.
The courses available are as Follows:
As per the present admission policy, 30% of the seats are under minority quota and rest of the seats will be filled by the Government as per
the norms of reservations in force. The seats under minority quota will be filled by merit in the qualifying examination.

Eligibility criteria

Selection of candidates is governed by the Rules and regulations laid down in this regard from time to time.
Candidates who wish to seek admission in any of the diploma courses will have to appear for common entrance examination (POLYCET)
conducted by the State Board of Technical Education and Training, Andhra Pradesh, Hyderabad.
Only the candidates satisfying the following requirements will be eligible to appear for the common entrance examination (POLYCET).

Documents Required at the time of Admission

For the candidate selected for admission the following documents are required to be furnished in original with two xerox copies of each.

Marks card of S.S.C
Transfer Certificate (at the time of admission only)
Conduct Certificate
Study Certificate IV to X
Residence Certificate
Physical Fitness Certificate
Caste Certificate if belongs to SC/ST and BC
Income Certificate
