Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Vision

To develop Electrical & Electronics Engineering professionals competent to face global challenges in an edifying environment that promotes technical knowledge, skills, ethics, and values to serve society for a better and more comfortable living.

Mission

M1: "To provide a competitive learning environment through a need-based curriculum designed in collaboration with industry, fostering high-quality education with an emphasis on knowledge transfer and skill development."

M2: "To nurture leadership qualities, ethics, and values in students, enabling them to excel in their chosen professions with integrity."

M3: "To encourage inquiry, innovation, and entrepreneurship while equipping students with life skills to handle challenges."

M4: "To promote industry and community collaboration for sustainable societal and environmental growth."
